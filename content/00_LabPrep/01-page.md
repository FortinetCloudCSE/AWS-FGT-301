## What to know before starting

> ##### FortiManager and FortiAnalyzer have been deployed as VMs within the individual labs.  The passwords have been set to 'Fortinet1234!'

&nbsp; 

> ##### FortiManager will have an "external facing" interface(port2) to simulate a publicly accessible FMG.  This would normally be done with a VIP or similar on an existing customer firewall.

&nbsp; 

> ##### Both Hub1 and Hub2 have their interface IPs configured and a static default route pointing to their underlay default gateway.  Both are also able to communicate with FMG via their MGMT IP address.
